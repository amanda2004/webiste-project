About Me

Hi, my name is Mandy! I live in Jakarta, where I am pursuing a bachelor degree in Information Systems. My passion for technology has always driven me to explore new possibilities, and this journey led me to focus on cloud computing, with a specific interest in becoming a skilled back-end developer. I love the idea of building complex applications and websites that solve real-world problems through logic and creativity.

Alongside my academic pursuits, I also have a creative side—I enjoy making content for TikTok and Instagram. Social media has become a fun way for me to share my interests and experiences with others, and it's one of my favorite hobbies.

One of my proudest achievements was winning the international competition “ACIYLS 2023,” where I represented Indonesia in Singapore. During the competition, I had the honor of leading a team to develop a start-up idea that tackled sustainable challenges. This experience sharpened my leadership and problem-solving skills and gave me valuable insights into the start-up world.

I also have hands-on experience in product management through a project called "Reminderoo," a subscription management platform that my team and I developed. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge further by joining the Cloud Computing path at Bangkit 2024, where I am learning cutting-edge technologies to advance my career in tech.

Projects
